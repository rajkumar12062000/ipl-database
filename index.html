Enjoy the game of IPL with us

Make your own team , Be your own master!!

Our application is to create your own team, add players to that, and decide the match results on your own. It will store all the data you entered into a database and will show the score table of all the teams you entered. Which includes points, win, loss, and net run rate, etc. The application contains four main options they are

This application uses Django backend with postgres Database which is similar like mySQL. This application was developed only as a personal project not for production.

This application is developed for desktop view not for mobile view, you need to open in desktop to access all contents.

  • Add player

    In this section, you can add players to a team on your own. You are asked to input the first name, last name, date of birth, nationality, and the team name to which you want to put that player. After filling in all the details click the submit button, our application is going to store all the details of that player in a database. And it will add the player to the team you selected. We have another two options in this section players list and back button. The Players list will take you to the list where you can see your added player's details. We have also provided a search bar where you can search the team and the number of players added to that team. And the back button will take you back to the home page.

  • Add Team

    In this section, you can add your own team and add players to that.You are asked to enter the team name and team color to create a team. Our application will store the data you entered safely in the database once you click the submit button.

  • Add Matches

    In this section, you can make clashes between teams and predict the results on your own. First, select two teams, and choose who won the toss and for what they opted for. Now predict the scores and overs taken by both the teams. Declare the result and click submit. This result will appear in the score table including the net run rate of a particular team.

  • Score Table

    This is the section where you can see all the team's performances. This includes the Logo of the team, matches played, points earned, number of wins vs losses, and the net run rate of the team.

  • Players List

    In this section, you can add players to a team on your own. You are asked to input the first name, last name, date of birth, nationality, and the team name to which you want to put that player. After filling in all the details click the submit button, our application is going to store all the details of that player in a database. And it will add the player to the team you selected. We have another two options in this section players list and back button. The Players list will take you to the list where you can see your added player's details. We have also provided a search bar where you can search the team and the number of players added to that team. And the back button will take you back to the home page.
